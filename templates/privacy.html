Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2023

At LegalEase, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when using our service:

  • Document Content: When you upload legal documents for analysis, we temporarily process the content to provide our services.
  • User Queries: Questions you ask about your documents are processed to provide relevant answers.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you access and use our service, including your IP address, browser type, and device information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process and analyze your legal documents
  • Respond to your questions and comments
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends
  • Protect against, identify, and prevent fraud and other illegal activity

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the security of your personal information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Document Retention

Your uploaded documents and extracted text are only stored temporarily during your active session. Once your session ends or after 24 hours (whichever comes first), all document data is automatically deleted from our servers. We do not maintain permanent copies of your documents.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Cloud's Gemini API to process and analyze legal documents. Your data is processed in accordance with Google's privacy policies and data protection standards. We do not share your personal information with any other third parties except as described in this privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete that information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@legalease.example.com.
